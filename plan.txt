ðŸ“‹ STEP 3: Image Implementation Rules
General Guidelines
â€‹
Always use next/image component:

jsx
import Image from "next/image"
Unsplash Source URL Pattern:

text
https://source.unsplash.com/{width}x{height}/?{keywords}
Always add unoptimized prop for Unsplash images:

jsx
<Image src="..." alt="..." width={800} height={600} unoptimized />
Use descriptive alt text (school context, not generic)

Maintain aspect ratios:

Hero: aspect-[16/9] or 1600x900

Cards: aspect-[4/3] or 800x600

Portraits: aspect-[3/4] or 600x800

Circular: 400x400

Consistent styling:

Rounded corners: rounded-2xl, rounded-3xl

Object fit: object-cover

Hover effects: group-hover:scale-105
